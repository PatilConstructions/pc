The final quality check is the culmination of all our quality control efforts. Punch List Rectification

During inspection, any issues found are documented on a "punch list" with specific details and photographs. Our team then systematically addresses each item:

  • Immediate Action ‚Äî Minor issues like paint touch-ups, door adjustments, or fixture tightening are fixed immediately on the same day.
  • Scheduled Repairs ‚Äî Larger items that require specific craftsmen or materials are scheduled with clear timelines.
  • Re-Inspection ‚Äî Every rectified item is re-inspected by the quality team to verify proper correction.
  • Sign-Off ‚Äî Only after all items are verified as complete is the project approved for handover.
