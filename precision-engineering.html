Advanced Surveying Techniques

Accurate surveying is the foundation of precision engineering. Before any construction begins, our survey team conducts a comprehensive site assessment using advanced equipment to establish accurate benchmarks and reference points. This data forms the basis for all subsequent construction activities.

üîß Our Surveying Equipment

Total Station: Electronic theodolite integrated with electronic distance measurement for highly accurate angle and distance measurements. Auto Level: Optical leveling instrument for establishing accurate horizontal planes and measuring elevation differences. Laser Level: Self-leveling rotary laser for quick and accurate level transfer across large areas. GPS Survey: Differential GPS for geo-referencing and large-scale topographical surveys.

  • Benchmark Establishment ‚Äî Permanent reference points are established at the site and verified against known survey benchmarks. All level measurements are referenced to these benchmarks throughout construction.
  • Setting Out ‚Äî Building lines, column positions, and foundation excavation limits are precisely marked using survey instruments. Multiple verification checks ensure accuracy before excavation begins.
  • Level Transfer ‚Äî Floor levels are transferred accurately from floor to floor using precise leveling instruments. Continuous monitoring ensures consistent floor heights throughout the building.
  • Plumb Verification ‚Äî Columns and walls are checked for vertical alignment using plumb bobs, theodolites, and laser plummets. Deviations are corrected immediately during construction.

  • Formwork Precision ‚Äî Column and beam formwork is fabricated and erected with precise dimensions and alignments. Formwork is checked and approved before concrete is poured.
