These premium-grade cements provide superior compressive strength, excellent workability, and long-term durability.

Fine Aggregates & Sand

The quality of sand and aggregates directly impacts the strength and durability of concrete and mortar. We use only clean, well-graded aggregates that are free from clay, silt, organic matter, and other impurities. Our sand is either washed river sand or high-quality manufactured sand (M-Sand) that meets IS 383 specifications.

üèîÔ∏è Our Aggregate Standards

Coarse Aggregates: Clean crushed stone aggregates of 20mm and 40mm nominal size from approved quarries. Properly graded with minimal flaky and elongated particles. Fine Aggregates: Zone II graded sand with fineness modulus between 2.6 and 2.9. Low silt content (less than 3%). Washed and screened for consistent quality.

We conduct sieve analysis, silt content tests, and bulking tests on every batch of sand received at site. Aggregates are properly stacked and protected from contamination. Different sizes are stored separately to maintain proper grading during use.
